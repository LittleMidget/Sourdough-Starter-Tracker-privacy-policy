Your Rights & Other Important Policies

What are my privacy rights?

Since we collect no personal data about you, there's no personal information to access, correct, or delete. Your sourdough starter tracking data is stored locally on your device and is fully under your control. You can clear this data by uninstalling the app.

How do you protect my data?

Your data protection is built into our design: we simply don't collect personal data. All your tracking data and settings are stored locally on your device using standard app data protection. No data leaves your device, so there's no data in transit to protect.

What about data retention?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you control data retention. Your sourdough starter tracking history and settings remain on your device until you delete them manually or uninstall the app. We don't retain any data about you on our servers because we don't collect any.

Is this app for children?

Yes, Sourdough Starter Tracker is suitable for users of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

How will I know if this policy changes?

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy here and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review it periodically.
